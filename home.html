They play soccer.

What should they do?

We made a low-level A.I. for our robots, that makes them play autonomously, without any help. They use many sensors to know where they are in the field and where the teammate, the ball and the goalpost are. Based on the sensors and the A.I they can choose what to do in a bunch of milliseconds.
